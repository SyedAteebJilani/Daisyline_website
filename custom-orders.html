Step 1 — Mandatory First Step

Contact Us First

Before submitting the custom order form, please reach out to our team via social channels or WhatsApp so we can review your idea, check yarn feasibility, and provide an accurate quotation.

Why contact us before ordering?

Custom crochet requires careful planning regarding colors, yarn texture, sizes, and artisan timeline. When you contact our designer first, we will:

  • Discuss your exact requirements
  • Check design feasibility
  • Finalize color & dimensions
  • Estimate production timeline
