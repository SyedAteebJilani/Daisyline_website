Why contact us before ordering?

Custom crochet requires careful planning regarding colors, yarn texture, sizes, and artisan timeline. When you contact our designer first, we will:

  • Discuss exact requirements
  • Check design feasibility
  • Finalize color & size
  • Estimate production time
