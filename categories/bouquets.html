The Anatomy of a Daisyline Bouquet.

True luxury isn't found in a factory line. It is woven petal by petal, loop by loop, over countless hours. We source only the finest, softest premium yarns to ensure that your bouquet isn't just a beautiful statement piece, but a lasting keepsake designed to bloom forever.

Premium Fibers

We use highly durable, color-fast cotton and soft velvet yarns.

Slow Made Process

Each bouquet takes several days to complete, ensuring absolute perfection in every petal and stem.

Heritage Inspired

Our patterns pay homage to traditional Pakistani craftsmanship and vintage crochet lore.
