The Anatomy of a Daisyline Bag.

True luxury isn't found in a factory line. It is woven stitch by stitch, loop by loop, over countless hours. We source only the finest, softest premium yarns to ensure that your accessory isn't just a beautiful statement piece, but a durable companion designed to last a lifetime.

Premium Fibers

Slow Made Process

Each bag takes several days to complete, ensuring absolute perfection in tension and shape.
