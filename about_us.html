Our Story

Every piece at Daisyline is handmade with love. We believe in the magic of slow living and the beauty of things crafted carefully, loop by loop, stitch by stitch.

From cozy cardigans to everlasting bouquets, we pay incredible attention to detail, ensuring that each piece is a unique creation made specially for our customers.

By choosing Daisyline, you are not just purchasing a product; you are supporting handmade craftsmanship and keeping a beautiful tradition alive.

Shipping Policy

  • Deliveries nationwide across Pakistan within 3–5 working days after crafting.
  • Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on location.
  • Tracking ID sent via SMS/Email as soon as parcel dispatches.

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 3 days of parcel delivery for damaged/defective items.
  • Unboxing video mandatory to process replacement or claim.
  • Custom-made items are strictly non-returnable once confirmed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How long does an order take to arrive?

Standard orders are dispatched in 3–5 working days. Custom items take 5–10 days depending on complexity.

What payment methods do you support?

We accept Easypaisa, JazzCash, and direct Bank Transfer. Advance payment screenshot upload is required.

Can I request color changes for standard items?

Yes! Simply select or mention your color preferences in the order notes or custom order form.
