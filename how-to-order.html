How to Order

Simple steps to order your handmade crochet treasures from Daisyline.

  1. Home
  2. /
  3. How to Order
1

Browse & Select

Explore our collections including Plushies, Bags, Bouquets, Baby Sets, and Keychains. Pick your favorite handcrafted items or choose custom specifications.

2

Add to Cart & Checkout

Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout. Provide your delivery address and contact phone number.

3

Advance Payment Verification

Since all items are individually handmade, we require advance payment to initiate crafting. Payment options include Easypaisa, JazzCash, and direct Bank Transfer. Attach your transaction screenshot at checkout or send via WhatsApp/Instagram.

4

Handcrafted with Care & Delivered

Once verified, our artisans begin crafting your order. standard items dispatch in 3–5 days, and custom items dispatch in 5–10 days with nationwide tracking provided.
